# Glade.ai Product Research

## Company Overview

**Mission:** "Glade.ai makes it easy to automate repetitive admin tasks, saving time, improving customer experience and increasing revenue."

**Tagline:** "Scale your practice. Not your team."

**Target Customers:**
- Bankruptcy law firms (primary)
- Immigration law firms
- General legal practices

**Company Stats:**
- Revenue: $2.4M (2025)
- Team: ~22 people
- Founded: 2022
- Location: Menlo Park, CA

---

## Core Product Features

### 1. AI Follow-up System
- Automated client communication via SMS
- Customizable follow-up cadence per client
- AI-driven reminders for document collection
- Configurable AI "tone of voice"
- 24/7 automated client support

### 2. Client Portal
- Self-service document upload
- E-signature capabilities
- Questionnaire completion
- Multi-language support
- Payment management

### 3. Document Checklist
- AI-powered document collection
- Tracks required documents per case type
- Automated follow-ups for missing docs
- "Document Collection with Accuracy in Mind"

### 4. Case Workflows
- Bankruptcy: Chapter 7, Chapter 13 workflows
- Immigration: Various visa/case types
- Modular workflow design
- Status tracking and customization
- Archive/unarchive capabilities

### 5. Invoices & Retainers
- Generate invoices within workflows
- Retainer agreement management
- E-signature for retainers
- Payment plan management
- Re-send/re-open capabilities

### 6. Consultations & Booking
- Client intake scheduling
- Prospective client management
- Booking widget for law firm websites

### 7. Credit Report
- Pull client credit reports securely
- Review within platform

### 8. Schedules Builder
- AI-powered form population for bankruptcy filings
- "AI Exemptions Agent" for schedule suggestions
- Income data organization for means test

### 9. eFiling Agent
- Prepare electronic filings
- Submit to court systems

## Pain Points Glade Solves

1. **Administrative burden** - Repetitive tasks automated
2. **Client follow-up** - AI handles reminder sequences
3. **Document collection** - Automated tracking and reminders
4. **Scaling without hiring** - Handle more clients with same team
5. **Client experience** - 24/7 responsiveness via AI
6. **Multilingual clients** - Platform translations

## Architecture Observations

- Multi-tenant SaaS (serves multiple law firms)
- Modular workflow system
- Integration with external services (Twilio SMS, credit bureaus, eFiling)
- Client portal separate from attorney dashboard
- Real-time task management and team collaboration
